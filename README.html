Refill by Studio30

What is Refill?

The flagship project for Studio30, Refill is a sink-attachable water bottle refilling station and filter which can provide the convenience of conventional bottle filling stations at a fraction of the cost

Why Does It Matter?

Refill is an opportunity for colleges and other institutions to encourage use of environmentally friendly reusable water bottles by increasing coverage and convenience of water bottle refilling options, without the outsize price tag usually attached.

About Us

Who We Are

We are a team of five Harvard College students who met as freshmen through breakdancing and decided, together, to make something new and solve a problem that directly affected our community.

What We Do

As Studio30, we provide a supportive environment where individuals can put forth ideas, knowing that there is a team of people with varied backgrounds who will provide positive support. Currently, we are working on Refill and aim to disrupt the water bottle refilling station industry.

Why Choose Us

We ourselves are college students. Thus, we understand the needs of our target market and have the ability to directly connect with a huge community.
